Ebola Threats at The Nigerian Land Borders

The situation at the Nigerian land borders poses a threat of possible re-entrance of Ebola Virus Disease. Based on observations in July during the on-set of the outbreak. This report shows our findings. Please watch and share your own experience or observations at any of the Nigerian land borders

Every 10mins 50 people cross into Nigeria through Idiroko, that borders Benin republic and Nigeria. However only about 15 of the 50 were screened for the Ebola Virus Disease because it was a random screening and scanning exercise. In order words 2160 people are screened daily at this border and the unscreened 5,040 people could be potential risk of Ebola and this figure can endanger the lives of 170 Million Nigerians
